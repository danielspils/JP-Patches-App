Here I take you through how to move a set of C/D banks from the library tab to
the active C/D banks in the JP Patches app. Then we send those same C/D banks
over to the JX-3P synth.

Other topics: custom patch names, drag-and-drop reorder, custom banks, patch
provenance … basically, everything patches!
